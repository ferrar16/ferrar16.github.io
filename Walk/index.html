Welcome to "Walk"

"Walk" is the serendipitous navigator that aims to aid you in your effort to discover and redefine the space in which you inhabit.

Slowly and Gradually

Through an introspective series of tasks and prompts, "Walk" acts as a catalyst to prod the user into new environments and headspaces.

Without Restraint.

"Walk" encourages the user to abandon their previous notions of navigation and rather than direct the user to a specific location, "Walk" allows the user to establish the destination for themself.

No data collected will be shared, period.

The idea of "Walk" is not to advance any sort of social or capitalistic gain, the app is solely meant for the introspective development of the user. To ensure this, we dedicate that no information recorded will be brokered to other companies.

This App is User Driven.

While most apps are set up to keep you engaged with notifications and reminders, this app is strictly user driven. We find that for the app to truly make an effect on the user, the effort has to come organically and without force.
